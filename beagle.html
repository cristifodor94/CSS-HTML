Beagel

Weight : 10 - 15Kg
Coat: Short haired, hard coat of medium lenght
Color: Many colors

The beagel is a breed of small hound that is similar in appereance to the much larger foxhound. The beagel is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare (beagling). With a great sense of smell and superior tracking instinct, the beagel is employed as detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagel is intellingent but single-minded. It is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and lack of inherited health problems.
